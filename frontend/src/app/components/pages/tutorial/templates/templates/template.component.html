Templates & Accompaniment

template meant as a notation template: instead of writing absolute notes*, you use relative degrees.
For example I4 means the first degree as a quarter note.

To transform these relative degrees into absolute notes it needs a chord.
Chords are defined in a sheet track.

*) although it's valid to write absolute notes into a template track

So with a chord we are able to determine absolute pitches. These pitches are the base material to resolve relative degree notes.
